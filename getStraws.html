CHOOSE YOUR OWN ADVETURE-MOE

Let your imagination go !

'Excuse me,' said the first little pig politely. 'Would you please sell some of your straw so I can make a house?' The man readily agreed and the first little pig went off to find a good place to build his house. In no time the little pig had built his straw house and set off to play.
One starlit night, soon after they had settled in, a wolf came out looking for food. By the light of the moon he espied the little pig’s house of straw and he sidled up to the door and called:
'Little pig, little pig, let me come in.'
'No, no, by the hair of my chinny chin chin!' replied the little pig.
' Then I’ll huff and I’ll puff and I’ll blow your house in!' said the wolf who was a very big, bad, and a greedy sort of wolf. And he huffed, and he puffed and blew the house in. But the little pig ran away as fast as his trotters could carry him. The scared little pig now wondered which of his brothers house to run to. His brother's stick and brick houses were not too far off.
