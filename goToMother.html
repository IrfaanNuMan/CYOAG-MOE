CHOOSE YOUR OWN ADVETURE-MOE

Let your imagination go !

In their panic the two little pigs descide to run to their mothers house. The rush past the blown down straw house and up the road. The big bad wolf is huffing and puffing as he chases the two little pigs. They can both feel the panting breath of the wolf on their trotters. The pigs realise that their mother's house is much further than their brothe's house made of bricks, but it's too late they must keep running now. They are starting to tire now and are running with laboured breaths squealing as the wolf gets even closer. They turn the corner and can see their mother's house perched on the hill.They shriek in both fair and joy as they get ever closer and can now make out the flowers blooming in their mother's garden.
The wolf realises that if they make it to the house he might lose out on his meal and he's desparately hungry. With one last huff he leaps snaring thelittle pigs and devouring them.
